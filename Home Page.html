Department Of CSE

Vision:

To produce globally competitive Computer Science engineers and entrepreneurs with moral values

Mission:

  • To achieve academic excellence through dedication and innovation in teaching-learning process.
  • To enrich learners of computer science and engineering into outstanding professionals with providing modern laboratories and facilities.
  • To acquire advanced knowledge through research activities in order to meet industrial and societal needs.

Program Outcomes:

  • Ability to apply the knowledge of mathematics, physical sciences and computer science and engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
  • Ability to identify, formulate and analyze complex real life problems in order to provide meaningful solutions by applying knowledge acquired in computer science and engineering.
  • Ability to design cost effective software / hardware solutions to meet desired needs of customers/clients.
